RekapPO - Smart Pre-Order Manager for UMKM

An app that helps micro pre order-based business owners who manage orders across platform manually to avoid missed order by providing simple system for tracking & prioritizing orders.
This app eliminates manual data entry by automatically parsing unstructured chat text and intelligently organizing incoming orders by their production deadlines.

✨ Core Features
- Automated Prioritization

  Automatic dashboard organizes itself every single day, bringing the most urgent orders to the very top.
- Simple Order Tracking

  A clean view of tracking order. Easily keep track of every customer, and move orders from “New” to “Done” with a single satisfying swipe
- Auto-fill Form From Text

  Instantly turn messy text into clean, organized order tickets. Just paste the customer’s message, and the app automatically extracts it into form

🛠 Tech Stack

Language : Swift

Frameworks : SwiftUI, SwiftData, Foundation

💡 The Problem It Solves

Small business owners struggle to manage their daily operations because they manually type or copy/paste customer orders from chatting app into basic notes or spreadsheets . As their business grows, this manual tracking becomes overwhelming, leading to missed deadlines and loss of customer trust.
